Hardle Rules

Guess the daily 4-letter word in 8 tries. Each guess must be a valid 4-letter word.

After each guess, a score (0-4) will be displayed, indicating how many letters from your guess are in the target word, regardless of position.

You can click on the letters to mark them as:

  • Orange: Letter could potentially be in the word
  • Green: Letter is definitely in the word (not necessarily in that position)
  • Red: Automatically applied to all letters in a guess that scores 0

Important: The game does not automatically indicate if a letter is in the correct position. Use the score and your deduction skills to solve the puzzle!
