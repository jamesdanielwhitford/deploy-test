Guess the four letter word

  • Every day, a new four-letter word is chosen for you to guess.
  • You have eight chances to guess the word correctly before you lose the game.
  • After each guess, you'll receive a score that indicates how many letters in your guess are also in the daily word.
  • If the daily word contains a double letter, such as "book" or "ball", and you guess a word that contains the same double letter, such as "bell", then the double letter will be counted as two separate matches.
  • Your previous guesses and scores will be displayed so you can keep track of your progress.
